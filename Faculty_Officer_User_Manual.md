# BUK CBT - Faculty Officer User Manual

Welcome, Faculty Officer. Your role is to bridge the gap between central administration and specific academic departments. You are responsible for managing the organizational structure and officer assignments within your assigned Faculty.

---

## 1. Dashboard Overview
Upon logging in, you will see the **Faculty Officer Dashboard**. This page provides a high-level statistical summary of your faculty:
-   **Departments:** Total number of departments under your faculty.
-   **Total Students:** Cumulative count of students across all your departments.
-   **Total Instructors:** Total number of lecturers and instructors assigned to your faculty.
-   **Total Exams:** The number of examinations (active and inactive) within your faculty.

---

## 2. Department Management

As a Faculty Officer, you are responsible for defining the departments within your faculty.

### 2.1. Viewing Departments
Navigate to the **"Departments"** link in the sidebar. You will see a list of all departments currently assigned to your faculty.

### 2.2. Adding a New Department
1.  On the Department Management page, click **"Add Department"**.
2.  Provide the following details:
    -   **Department Name:** The official name (e.g., "Department of Software Engineering").
    -   **Head of Department (HOD):** The name of the current HOD.
    -   **Description:** A brief overview of the department.
    -   **Contact Information:** Official email and phone number for the department.
3.  Click **"Create Department"**. The department will automatically be linked to your faculty.

### 2.3. Editing or Deactivating Departments
-   Use the **"Edit"** button on any department card to update information.
-   You can toggle the status between **Active** and **Inactive**.

---

## 3. Officer Management (Dept Officers)

Once a department is created, you must assign a **Department Officer** (also known as a Level Admin) to manage its daily tasks (registering students, lecturers, etc.).

### 3.1. Creating a Department Officer
1.  Navigate to **"Dept Officers"** (Admin Management) in the sidebar.
2.  Click **"Create New"**.
3.  Fill in the officer's **Full Name** and **Email Address**.
4.  Select the **"Department Officer"** role.
5.  **Important:** Choose the specific department from the dropdown list. You will only see departments belonging to your faculty.
6.  Click **"Create"**.
7.  Provide the officer with their login credentials (default password is usually `password`).

### 3.2. Managing Officers
-   You can reset an officer's password if they lose access.
-   You can update their status or delete the account if they are no longer in that role.

---

## 4. Academic & Exam Oversight

While Department Officers handle the creation of exams and students, you have full visibility into these activities for oversight.

### 4.1. Monitoring Exams
Navigate to the **"Exams"** page to see all submitted and active exams across your faculty. You can monitor their progress, view assigned invigilators, and see exam durations.

### 4.2. Viewing Student Records
Navigate to the **"Students"** page to view the list of all students registered in your faculty's departments. You can use the search bar to find specific students by name or candidate number.

### 4.3. Reviewing Archives
The **"Archives"** section allows you to view the results of concluded exams. You can drill down into specific archived exams to see student performance and final scores for any department in your faculty.

---

## 5. Support & Settings
-   **Settings:** You can change your own profile details and password in the Settings page.
-   **Manual:** Access this guide at any time by clicking "User Manual" at the bottom of your sidebar.
