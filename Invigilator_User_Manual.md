# BUK CBT - Invigilator User Manual

Welcome, Invigilator. Your role is critical for maintaining the integrity and smooth operation of the examination process. This manual outlines your responsibilities on the day of the exam.

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1. Login
Log in using the email and password provided to you by the system administrator.

### 1.2. Assigned Exam
Upon login, you will be presented with the dashboard showing the **active exam session** you have been assigned to invigilate. All your actions will be in the context of this single active exam.

If you do not see an active exam, it means the administrator has not yet activated it or assigned you to it.

## 2. Invigilation Tasks: Step-by-Step

### 2.1. Viewing Enrolled Students
-   From your dashboard, you can view the list of all students enrolled in the active exam.
-   This list will show each student's name, candidate number, and their current check-in status.

### 2.2. Checking In Students
This is your most important pre-exam task. A student **cannot log in** to the exam until you have checked them in.

1.  Find the student in your list.
2.  Verify their identity using their official ID card and by comparing it to their photo on your screen.
3.  Once verified, click the **"Check-In"** button for that student.
4.  The student's status will update, and they will now be permitted to log in using their Candidate Number and a valid Ticket Number.

### 2.3. Monitoring the Exam
During the exam, you can monitor student activity from your dashboard. This includes:
-   Seeing who has successfully logged in.
-   Viewing security violation flags triggered by students (e.g., leaving fullscreen, switching tabs).

### 2.4. Managing Student Issues
During the exam, you may need to perform certain administrative actions.

#### Extending Time
If a student requires an extension due to technical issues or other valid reasons, you can grant them additional time.
1.  Find the student on your dashboard.
2.  Click the **"Extend Time"** button.
3.  Enter the number of **additional minutes** to grant the student.
4.  Confirm the action. The student's exam timer will be updated in real-time.

*This functionality may also be available to administrators.*

## 3. Post-Exam Actions

### 3.1. Terminating the Exam
This action is typically performed by a **System Administrator**, but may be delegated to the head invigilator in some cases. Terminating the exam will:
-   End the session for all active students.
-   Archive the exam results.
-   Clear all active tickets and student login statuses.

**Warning:** Only perform this action if you are explicitly instructed to do so. It is irreversible for the current session.
