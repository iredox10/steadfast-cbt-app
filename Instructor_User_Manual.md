# BUK CBT - Instructor/Lecturer User Manual

Welcome, Instructor. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of your responsibilities and capabilities within the BUK CBT platform. Your primary role is to manage exams and questions for the courses you are assigned.

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1. Login
Log in using the email and password provided to you by the system administrator. Upon your first login, it is highly recommended to change your password.

### 1.2. Dashboard
Your dashboard provides a quick overview of your assigned courses, active exams, and recent activities.

## 2. Managing Your Courses & Exams

You can only manage exams for courses that have been assigned to you by an administrator.

### 2.1. Viewing Assigned Courses
Navigate to the "My Courses" section to see a list of all courses assigned to you.

### 2.2. Creating an Exam
1.  Navigate to the desired course.
2.  Select the option to **"Create Exam"**.
3.  Fill in the exam details:
    -   **Exam Title:** A descriptive title (e.g., "Mid-Semester Examination").
    -   **Exam Duration:** The total time for the exam, in minutes (e.g., `60`).
    -   **Number of Questions to Serve:** The number of questions that will be presented to each student from the question bank.
    -   **Marks Per Question:** The score value for each correct answer.
    -   **Instructions:** Provide any specific instructions for students taking the exam.

### 2.3. Activating and Terminating Exams
-   **Activation:** An exam must be activated by an **Admin** before students can take it. You will need to coordinate with the administration for exam scheduling.
-   **Termination:** Once the exam period is over, an **Admin** will terminate the session.

## 3. Managing Questions

The core of your role is creating and managing the questions for your exams.

### 3.1. Adding Questions to an Exam
1.  From the exam management page, select **"Add/View Questions"**.
2.  Click **"Add New Question"**.
3.  You will be presented with a form to enter:
    -   **The Question:** The question text. You can use formatting if needed.
    -   **Options:** Enter the text for each of the multiple-choice options (Option A, B, C, D).
    -   **Correct Answer:** Select the radio button corresponding to the correct answer.
4.  Save the question. It will be added to the question bank for that specific exam.

### 3.2. Editing or Deleting Questions
-   From the question list for an exam, you can click the **"Edit"** icon to modify an existing question and its options.
-   You can also delete a question if it is no longer needed.

### 3.3. Question Bank
The system maintains a question bank for each exam. When an exam is active, the system will randomly select the specified "Number of Questions to Serve" from this bank for each student, ensuring a varied exam experience.

## 4. Monitoring and Results

### 4.1. Viewing Student Submissions
After students have completed an exam, you can navigate to the "Results" or "Submissions" section for that exam to view:
-   A list of all students who took the exam.
-   The score of each student.

### 4.2. Exporting Scores
You have the option to export the final scores for your course into an Excel file for your records or for uploading to other university portals. Look for the **"Export to Excel"** button on the results page.
