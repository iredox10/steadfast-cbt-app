# BUK CBT - Student User Manual

Welcome to the BUK Computer-Based Testing (CBT) platform. This guide will walk you through the process of taking an exam, from logging in to submitting your answers.

## 1. Before You Begin

Before you can take an exam, you must:
1.  Be officially registered as a student on the platform by an administrator.
2.  Be enrolled in the course for which the exam is being held.
3.  Be checked in by an invigilator for the specific exam session.
4.  Receive a unique **Ticket Number** for the exam session.

**Required for Login:**
-   Your official **Candidate Number** (e.g., your student ID).
-   The **Ticket Number** provided for the current exam.

## 2. Taking an Exam: Step-by-Step

### Step 2.1: Login
On the student login page, enter your **Candidate Number** and the **Ticket Number** you received.

### Step 2.2: Exam Instructions
After a successful login, you will be taken to the **Exam Instructions** page. This page contains vital information about the exam:
-   **Course Title and Code:** Make sure you are taking the correct exam.
-   **Exam Duration:** The total time allotted for the exam.
-   **Total Questions:** The number of questions you will need to answer.
-   **Marks per Question:** The value of each question.
-   **Security Rules:** Read these carefully. The system monitors for violations like exiting fullscreen, switching tabs, or trying to copy/paste. Reaching the maximum violation limit will result in automatic submission of your exam.

Read all instructions thoroughly before proceeding.

### Step 2-3: Starting the Exam
Once you have read and understood the instructions, click the **"Start Examination"** button to begin. The exam timer will start immediately after you click this button.

### Step 2.4: The Exam Interface
The exam page is divided into three main sections:

1.  **Header Bar (Top):**
    -   Displays your name, candidate number, and photo.
    -   Shows the course title and the number of questions.
    -   Features a **live countdown timer**. Your exam will automatically submit when the timer reaches zero.
    -   Includes a **Calculator** button to open a draggable on-screen calculator for your use.

2.  **Question Navigator (Left Sidebar):**
    -   A grid of numbers representing all the questions in the exam.
    -   **Blue:** The current question you are viewing.
    -   **Green:** A question you have already answered.
    -   **Gray:** A question you have not yet answered.
    -   You can click any number to jump directly to that question.

3.  **Question & Answer Area (Main Area):**
    -   Displays the current question text.
    -   Shows the multiple-choice options (A, B, C, D).
    -   To select an answer, simply click on the option. The selected option will be highlighted.
    -   Use the **"Previous"** and **"Next"** buttons at the bottom to navigate sequentially through the questions.

### Step 2.5: Answering Questions
-   Read each question carefully.
-   Click on your desired answer from the list of options.
-   Your answer is saved automatically as soon as you select it. You can change your answer at any time before submitting the exam by simply selecting a different option.

### Step 2.6: Submitting the Exam
You have two ways to submit the exam:
-   **Automatic Submission:** The exam will automatically submit when the timer runs out.
-   **Manual Submission:** If you finish early, you can click the **"Submit Exam"** button. A confirmation pop-up will appear to prevent accidental submission. Confirm your choice to finish the exam.

**Important:** Once you submit the exam, you cannot go back.

## 3. After Submission
After your exam is submitted, you will be directed to one of two pages, depending on the system configuration:
-   **Submission Confirmation Page:** A page confirming that your exam has been successfully received.
-   **Results Page:** A page displaying your score, the number of correct answers, and other performance details (if enabled by the administrator).
